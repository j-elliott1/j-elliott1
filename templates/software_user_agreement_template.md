# Software User Agreement Template

This User Agreement documents the terms, conditions, and responsibilities associated with accessing and using organization-managed software.  
It serves as an auditable acknowledgment that the user understands and agrees to comply with all software, security, and acceptable use policies.

## Agreement Terms

By signing this agreement, I acknowledge and agree to the following:

1. **Authorized Use Only:**  
   I will use this software solely for official business purposes and within the scope of my job responsibilities.

2. **License Compliance:**  
   I will not install, copy, transfer, or share this software or its license key beyond the terms permitted by the organization or vendor license.

3. **Security & Data Protection:**  
   I will not disable, modify, or circumvent any security configurations related to this software.  
   I will handle all data processed through the software in accordance with company security and privacy policies.

4. **Prohibited Actions:**  
   I will not use this software for personal projects, external consulting, or any unauthorized third-party purposes.  
   I will not export or transfer the software to another device without prior approval.

5. **Incident Reporting:**  
   I will report any unauthorized use, suspected license violations, or software malfunctions to IT or the SAM team immediately.

6. **Software Removal:**  
   Upon termination of my employment, contract, or reassignment, I understand that access will be revoked and the software may be uninstalled from my system.

7. **Monitoring and Audit:**  
   I understand that my software usage may be monitored and audited to ensure compliance with company and vendor requirements.
