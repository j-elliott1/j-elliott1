# Vendor Software Usage Agreement Template

This agreement outlines the terms and responsibilities under which an external vendor, contractor, or service provider may access or use software, systems, or digital assets owned or licensed by the organization.  
It ensures compliance with licensing, security, and confidentiality requirements while protecting organizational interests.

## ⚙️ Terms and Conditions

By signing this agreement, the vendor acknowledges and agrees to the following:

1. **Authorized Use Only:**  
   Access to organization-managed software or systems is strictly limited to the scope defined in this agreement and associated contract.

2. **Compliance with Licensing Terms:**  
   Vendor must use software in accordance with the vendor’s EULA and the organization’s Software Asset Management (SAM) policies.

3. **Non-Transferability:**  
   Vendor may not share, sublicense, or redistribute software access credentials to any unauthorized personnel.

4. **Security Obligations:**  
   - Vendor must maintain up-to-date antivirus, endpoint protection, and security controls on all devices used to access organizational systems.  
   - Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) must be enabled wherever supported.  
   - Vendor must immediately report any security incidents or data breaches.

5. **Confidentiality:**  
   - Vendor shall not disclose or use any proprietary information, data, or intellectual property outside the scope of their engagement.  
   - All data accessed or processed under this agreement remains the exclusive property of the organization.

6. **Monitoring and Audit:**  
   - The organization reserves the right to audit software usage, log activity, and revoke access at any time.  
   - Vendor agrees to cooperate fully with audit or review activities related to licensing, security, or compliance.

7. **Termination of Access:**  
   - Access will be revoked upon contract completion, termination, or breach of policy.  
   - Vendor must confirm removal of any local copies or cached data within 48 hours of termination.

8. **Liability and Indemnification:**  
   Vendor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the organization from any claims, damages, or costs resulting from misuse or breach of this agreement.
